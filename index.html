What is TarkaVeda?

TarkaVeda is an independent initiative that investigates and verifies Ayurveda-related claims circulating online. We combine classical Ayurvedic sources with modern scientific research to offer a rational, respectful approach to holistic health.

How We Fact-Check

  1. Claim Detection: We track viral wellness posts or user-submitted claims.
  2. Source Evaluation: We consult Ayurveda texts and scientific journals.
  3. Response: We publish an honest, cited review.
